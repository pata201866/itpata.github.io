Alibaba established its own logistics service company

2018-06-10T21:08:43+08:00

Alibaba's logistics service platform, Cainiao, established a new company , hangzhou miaodi zhaipei technology .According to TianYanCha.com, the register was approved on Feb 24 2018.

It is interesting to know that Alibaba finally decided to acknowledge one of the biggest mistakes that Alibaba has made since its foundation,logistics

Alibaba's co-founder and chairman Jack Ma made a point of not build its own logistics service system publicly several years ago. At that time,the biggest competitor ,in terms of online shopping,JD.com had been investing a lot to its own logistics service .And the efforts that JD made pays off.Nowadays, many Chinese consumers prefer JD to Tmall or Taobao and the quality logistics service that sellers offer is often the biggest reason that influence them.

How can Alibaba not know that logistics service is critical to user experience ? Lack of quality logistics service will do harm to all Alibaba's platform, such as Tmall and Taobao,which account for its main revenues. Apparently, Alibaba won't let that happen.But Jack Ma clearly knows that his company is quite good at building platforms and is not armed with the experience of building a delivering service from the scratch.Also,it takes money and time.

Alibaba came up with a plan ,which is Cainiao.When Chinese consumers heard about the news ,they felt puzzled,or even doubt.Cainiao is a platform that integrate several big logistics companies in China,including shentong,zhongtong shunfeng and others.In fact ,those are the key players in China's logistics industry.Shunfeng is probably the most preferred logistics company in China.
