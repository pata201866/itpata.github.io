Kuaishou acquired the once dying ACG video platform AcFun

June 5, 2018, 11:44 p.m.

Kuaishou has completed the acquisition of AcFun,According to Chinese tech media 36kr today.After going around many business talks with potential buyers,AcFun,a once-popular Chinese video platform,has finally grasped the chance to survive the firece competition of its strong rivals of Chinese video sites. Details of the specific transactions remains unknown.36kr received the information from multiple sources.When the tech media asked Kuaishou about the deal,it got the confirmations of the acquisition from the company.It is estimated that the price of Kuaishou's acquisition would be more than 750 million yuan(around 117 million dollars).In fact,rumors spreaded that Kuaishou had been considering a lead investment in AcFun.But the investment had to be canceled when Kuaishou had tried to find other investors interested in AcFun but received no intentions.
