Douyin and Toutiao critisized by state media again

June 6, 2018, 11:11 p.m.

June 6,2018.It was found that Douyin mistakenly took advantage of a Chinese hero who was sent to North Korea during the Korean War and sacrificed himself in one of the bloodiest battles.A Weibo user posted Douyin's misbehavior on Weibo and the content was soon after shared by many people.

Weibo is a popoular social media microblogging service platform.It has long been regarded as China's Twitter,which is banned in China.Weibo has made a lot of customizations and added a lot of different features which meet the needs of Chinese users.Alibaba is one of Weibo's main inverstors and perhaps is the most important one.

Today a Weibo user accidently found that when Douyin advertise itself on Sougou search engine ranking pages,it made fun of a Qiu shaoyun,a household name in China and has been seen as a hero.

In the beginning,the Weibo user thought that it was the Sougou search engine that may be to blame.

However,when he tapped the ad,he was taken to a landing page,on top of which Douyin's banner that clearly says "jokes about Qiu shaoyun on fire",it is evident that Douyin is the one that is to blame.

That piece of weibo is widespread shared and Douyin,the popular short video platform soon got critisized by many people.After around 2 hours,Toutiao,or Bytedance,company that produced Douyin,released a an official apology statement.

The apology is listed below.

1.We sincerely apologize to the public and the bereaved family for the disrespectful content in search engine advertising.This is a very serious blunder. We deeply apologize and will reflect on the negative social impact.

2.The advertising team commissioned an advertising company in Beijing,which is a serious mistake in terms of placing advertisements on a search engine platform.We did not carefully reviewed the keywords packages provided by third parties.We failed to filter the content.We are at fault.

3.At present,the offensive ads have all been suspended.We will deeply reflect on and carefully review the process to find the loopholes,and seriously make changes.The general manager of the marketing team and the project leader will suspend their roles in Toutiao.

4.We are full of reverence and gratitude for the contributions that heroes have made to the People's Republic of China.Any ridicule for the desecration of heroes is intolerable,and it is a serious violation of the values we hold.We will launch an education of hero and thanksgiving,to prevent the recurrence of such events.And let that be cemented into the psyche.

It not the first time that Toutiao has been through this kind of things.Baozoumanhua,or Rage Comic had been take advantage of its Toutiao account to deliver videos that showed no respect to a Chinese hero who went to join the Korean War and never came back.Baozoumanhua soon got critisized by China's state media and propagandists and saw its social accounts deleted by various platforms,including Weibo,Zhihu,and of course ,Toutiao.

Toutiao was also severely criticized,as the content platform failed to take its responsibility.

Bytedance has grown fast over the past 2 years.Starting form a news app that adopts machine learning technology and recommendation algorithm,it quickly attracted hundreds of millions of users.After that,it released short video and music video app,Douyin,and a Q&A website.Bytedance acquired musical.ly,the popular video and music app in north america not long ago.Ventures have been chasing after it and even internet giants like Tencent and Alibaba would like it to be their partner.It seems that Bytedance's strong growth also cloaks a more complicated picture which might hurts itself every now and then.

Anyway,it is good to have Toutiao here,who challenges Baidu,Tencent and Alibaba and brings something new to Chinese Internet battle field.
